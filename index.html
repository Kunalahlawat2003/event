About Us

DPG Institute of Technology and Management is one of the best managed self-financed Colleges of Haryana, imparting quality technical education using most modern techniques. The institute gives top priorities to interactive dialogue with students on one to one basis. The ongoing online interaction between students and teachers as well as confidential feed back from the students on each course in every semester is the hallmark of DPG Institute of Technology and Management Education System. The institute ensures that every student is skilled in all aspects when they are moved to work in the industry.

Get ready to unleash your scientific curiosity at the "Vigyan Utsav" science fair, hosted by the DPG Institute of Technology & Management on November 4th, 2023. This exciting event promises to be a platform for budding scientists, innovators, and tech enthusiasts to showcase their creative projects and experiments. From cutting-edge robotics to eco-friendly solutions, the Vigyan Utsav will be a melting pot of ingenious ideas and scientific exploration. Join us for a day of awe-inspiring discoveries and a celebration of the spirit of innovation. Mark your calendars and be a part of this inspiring journey into the world of science and technology!

To participate in the "Vigyan Utsav" science fair at the DPG Institute of Technology & Management on November 4th, 2023, registration is a straightforward process. To get started, visit Vigyan Utsav's official website and navigate to the registration section. There, you'll find a Google form tailored for your respective school. Fill out the form with your project details and essential information, and you're on your way to being a part of this exciting scientific event. Don't miss this opportunity to showcase your scientific talents and innovations at Vigyan Utsav – the first step is just a few clicks away!

Our college is not only a hub for academic excellence but also a platform for a diverse range of events that cater to students' interests and talents. In addition to hosting the "Vigyan Utsav" science fair, we also celebrate Engineer's Day with great enthusiasm, honoring the engineering pioneers who have shaped our world. This day is marked by inspiring talks, interactive workshops, and various engineering-related activities that engage and educate our students. Furthermore, we embrace the world of gaming through our annual Vortex gaming event, where students can showcase their gaming prowess, strategic thinking, and sportsmanship. These events, along with many others, create a holistic learning environment that fosters both academic and extracurricular growth.

At our college, innovation is at the core of our academic pursuits. We take pride in our commitment to fostering creativity and technological advancements. Our students and faculty have collaborated on numerous groundbreaking projects, such as the development of a cutting-edge heart rate sensor, ultrasonic shoes for the visually impaired, and an ingenious auto writing machine. These initiatives showcase our dedication to pushing the boundaries of technology and engineering. We invite you to explore these remarkable achievements in the "Our Projects" section of our website to witness the innovative spirit that thrives within our institution.
